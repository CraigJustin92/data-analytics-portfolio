HR Employee Attrition Dashboard

Analyzing factors influencing turnover using Tableau

🔍 Overview

This project investigates key drivers behind employee attrition using Tableau. By leveraging interactive dashboards, HR teams can quickly pinpoint problem areas in job satisfaction, workload, and department-level turnover trends.

🧠 Key Findings

  • Overtime employees are over 3x more likely to leave the company.
  • Low job satisfaction directly correlates with high attrition rates.
  • Department and role stagnation often lead to disengagement.

📊 Tools Used

Tableau, Excel, Python (for preprocessing)
